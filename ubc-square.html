Premium Commercial & Residential Mix

UBC Square, located at the entry point of Mangalore, is a super luxury apartment ideally situated in the most happening part of the city at Kankanady Bypass Road. This 6-storey residential building offers well-planned and spacious 24 units of 2BHK apartments for comfortable living.

Designed with a focus on "Perfect Finishing," UBC Square stands as a testament to our commitment to providing quality houses and commercial spaces in a peaceful environment.

Specifications

  • Superior 800mm x 800mm vitrified tiles flooring
  • Antiskid ceramic tile for bathroom flooring
  • Glazed tiles for bathroom wall cladding
  • Designed glazed tiles for kitchen wall
  • Granite kitchen platform with stainless steel sink
  • Branded sanitary fittings (Jaguar/ Johnson)
  • Cables (V-guard/ RR) & Branded Switches
  • CPVC for hot water (Ashirvad)
  • UPVC or PVC for warm water (Supreme/ Dutron)
