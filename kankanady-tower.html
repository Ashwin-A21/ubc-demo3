The New Commercial Landmark

Kankanady Tower is a premium commercial development designed to cater to the modern needs of retail, office, and business establishments. Located in the high-visibility area of Kankanady, Opp. Ganesh Medical, it stands as a 5-storey landmark (Basement + Ground + 5 Floors).

Designed with architectural precision and a focus on functionality, Kankanady Tower offers a prestigious address for businesses looking to establish themselves in the heart of Mangalore.

Project Specifications

  • RCC Framed Structure
  • Premium Vitrified Tiles for Shops and Offices
  • Granite flooring for Common areas & Staircases
  • S.S Railings for all staircases
  • Branded Switches and Modular Electrical setup
  • Superior Putty finish for interior walls
  • UPVC/Aluminium windows for commercial units
  • High-visibility glass frontages for retail units
  • Melamine or PU coated entrance doors for offices
  • Provision for Inverter point in each unit

Building Amenities

  • High-speed automated elevators
  • Reliable 24x7 water supply from city & borewell
  • Full DG backup for common areas and units
  • Ample parking space in basement & ground level
  • CCTV surveillance for all common corridors
  • Firefighting systems as per national norms
  • Provision for split AC in all shops and offices
  • LED light fittings for common areas
  • Access controlled entry for office floors
  • Designated signage areas for each establishment
